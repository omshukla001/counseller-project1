Knowledge Park 360 — SRM & Bangalore Engineering Admission Consultancy

Knowledge Park 360 is an independent admission consultancy supporting students who are applying to SRM Institute of Science and Technology (Chennai) and leading engineering colleges in Bangalore — including RV College of Engineering (RVCE), BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE), MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology (MSRIT), PES University, BMS Institute of Technology (BMSIT), Bangalore Institute of Technology (BIT), Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering (DSCE), Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology (NMIT), RNS Institute of Technology (RNSIT), Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology (Sir MVIT), and Jain (Deemed-to-be University). We are not affiliated with or authorised representatives of any of these institutions. With over six years of admission counselling experience, our team helps families shortlist colleges, understand KCET, COMEDK, SRMJEE, JET, and PESSAT cutoffs, and navigate counselling rounds.

Admission Counselling Services

We offer admission counselling for engineering aspirants across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. Our counsellors guide families through KCET counselling, COMEDK UGET seat allotment, SRMJEE Phase 1 and Phase 2 application processes, management quota and other quota-based admission routes (subject to each college's policy), scholarship eligibility, documentation support, and branch selection across Computer Science (CSE), Information Science (ISE), Electronics and Communication (ECE), Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML), Data Science, Cyber Security, Mechanical, Civil, and Biotechnology programs. We do not guarantee admission to any institution.
